## Musical Journey

- Sharik Khan Jr ne apna musical career hip hop rap genre se start kiya.
- Unka sabse popular rap song "Are Kya Bolti Meri Achalpur Public" (2024) hai, aur kai tracks sabhi music platforms par available hain.
